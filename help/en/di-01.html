HEADQUARTERS (HQ)

  • HQ Benefits
  • HQ Level Up Rewards

This is the core Base structure. From it you can not only check your city Defenses and active Boosters, but also use some if needed. Additionally when upgrading your Headquarters you are able to unlock new Add-on slots, District Improvements and increase your base building speed permanently.
